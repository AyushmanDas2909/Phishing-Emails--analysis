### Grammatical and Usage Mistakes



\- \*\*Error Type:\*\* Contraction/Possessive Error and Subject-Verb Agreement

&nbsp;   - \*\*Incorrect:\*\* Its important that you're corporate accounts is secure.

&nbsp;   - \*\*Correction:\*\* It's important that your corporate accounts are secure.

&nbsp;   - \*\*Explanation:\*\* Should be \*It's\* (It is), not \*Its\* (possessive), and \*your\* (possessive), not \*you're\* (you are). The plural subject \*accounts\* requires the plural verb \*are\*, not \*is\*.



\- \*\*Error Type:\*\* Redundant Punctuation

&nbsp;   - \*\*Incorrect:\*\* To secure your account and re-activate your full access privilages, please clik on the secure link below...

&nbsp;   - \*\*Correction:\*\* To secure your account and re-activate your full access privileges please click on the secure link below...

&nbsp;   - \*\*Explanation:\*\* The comma before "please" is unnecessary after the introductory phrase.



\- \*\*Error Type:\*\* Inconsistency/Styling

&nbsp;   - \*\*Incorrect:\*\* Email Attachments- Urgent\_Security\_Patch.exe

&nbsp;   - \*\*Correction:\*\* Email Attachments: Urgent\_Security\_Patch.exe

&nbsp;   - \*\*Explanation:\*\* A colon is more standard than a dash for introducing a list or item.
